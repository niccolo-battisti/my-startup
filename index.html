Our mission

Heartcare is a brand new startup that consists in an overhaul of the personal healthcare system, giving to old people and their family a new mean to take care of theirselves. Our purpose is to make people feel healthier and happier, while sharing good moments in the privacy of their own home.

What Heartcare offers

Our startup offers a great variety of services and goods to support people in their daily lives to build personalized workout plans, dietary advice, an integration with the medical tests performed, sensors to call for help in case of physical imbalances, and a local presence through our active colleagues in some of the European cities.
